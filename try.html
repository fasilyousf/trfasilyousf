Institute at a Glance

Located in the heart of Ucchandwara, our institute has been a beacon of quality education for over 15 years. We offer job-oriented computer courses, vocational training, and academic support to students from rural and semi-urban areas.

With a mission to bridge the digital divide, we provide hands-on training, experienced faculty, and a supportive learning environment. Our alumni work in top IT firms and government sectors.
